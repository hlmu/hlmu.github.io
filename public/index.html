I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, focusing on Natural Language Processing, supervised by Prof. Wanxiang Che. My expertise lies in Dialogue Assistants and Audio Processing. Currently my research interests includes LLM Agents and Evaluation. Skilled in Python, C/C++, and Java, I also have professional experience as a Test Development Engineer at Tencent Technology. Passionate about AI, I am committed to advancing the field of NLP through rigorous research and practical applications.
